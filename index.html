About Us

911 photography is owned and managed by Owolanke Kolawole. Kolawole has been one of the most iconic figures in contemporary Photography for more than five years. Born in Ondo, Nigeria, Kolawole started photography while in the university.

Kolawole has been recognized with few awards in the industry and he has major photography deals with both private and public parastatals across Nigeria.
